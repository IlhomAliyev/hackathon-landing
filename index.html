About

Our hackathon will be held in a hybrid format, in which participants need to team up to create a project together using various technologies and design and development tools.

The hackathon takes place over five days, during which we will provide you with all the necessary information and support to help you create a unique project. And the main prize of 10 million soums will be a worthy reward for your hard work.

Task

As part of our hackathon, you and a team consisting of designers and developers will have to to develop an online service in the field of B2B (business to business), about which you will learn in more detail at an offline opening.

Please make sure that your team is fully formed before the end of the registration period (Date). It should include six specialists, one of whom will also be captain of the team.

Important: the registration of a certain team on this website is handled only by the captain.

In case you still don't have your own team, then feel free go to our Telegram chat, where you can join an already formed team, so create your own.

But hurry up, the number of teams at the hackathon is strictly limited!
